1. The "Community Impact" Layer (YGA Style)
Upvoting/Me-Too System: Instead of 10 different people reporting the same broken light, let users "Upvote" an existing report. This helps the admin see which issues are affecting the most people.

"Fixed" Hall of Fame: A section showing "Before & After" photos of resolved issues. This builds trust in the system and encourages more students to participate.

Gamification (Safety Points): Reward "Safety Heroes" with points or badges for reporting valid issues. These could potentially be traded for coffee at campus cafes or "Social Responsibility" credits.
